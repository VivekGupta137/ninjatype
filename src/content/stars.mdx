# ‚≠ê Star Rating System

As you practice typing with each finger, you'll earn stars based on your typing speed (WPM) and accuracy. The star system is designed to reward consistent improvement while maintaining good accuracy.

## How to Earn Stars

**Base Stars Calculation:**

Stars are earned based on your typing speed using a simple formula:

**Stars = WPM √∑ 2** (rounded down)

**Example:** If you type at 14 WPM, you earn 7 base stars (14 √∑ 2 = 7)

### Star Progression by WPM

- **1-10 WPM:** 0-5 stars
- **11-20 WPM:** 5-10 stars
- **21-30 WPM:** 10-15 stars
- **31-40 WPM:** 15-20 stars
- **41+ WPM:** 20+ stars
